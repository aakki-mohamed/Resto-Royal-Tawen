About Royal Tawen

Welcome to Royal Tawen, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you the finest dining experience, blending traditional flavors with modern culinary techniques.

At Royal Tawen, we believe in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that not only tantalize your taste buds but also nourish your body and soul. Our expert chefs pour their passion and creativity into every meal, ensuring that each dish is a masterpiece of flavor and presentation.

From our signature bibimbap to our carefully curated selection of international cuisines, we offer a diverse menu that caters to all palates. Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or a business lunch, our attentive staff is committed to making your visit memorable.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Mohamed Aakki

Executive Chef

With over 15 years of culinary experience, Chef Mohamed Aakki brings her passion for innovative cuisine to Royal Tawen. Trained in both traditional Korean cooking and modern gastronomy, she expertly blends flavors and techniques to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Chef Aakki's philosophy revolves around using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients to craft dishes that are both comforting and exciting. Her dedication to culinary excellence has earned Royal Tawen numerous accolades and a loyal following of food enthusiasts.
